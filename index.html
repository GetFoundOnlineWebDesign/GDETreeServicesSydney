Canopy Lifts, Palm Maintenance & Preventative Tree Work

Not every job is a full removal. Regular maintenance by experienced tree professionals helps keep trees healthier and reduces the risk of storm damage and limb failures.

  • Canopy lifting to increase sunlight into homes, lawns and gardens
  • Thinning and weight reduction in heavy crowns to reduce wind loading
  • Pruning to clear roofs, gutters, solar panels, driveways and fences
  • Palm cleaning to remove dead fronds and messy seed pods over pools and outdoor areas
  • Shaping and formative pruning for younger trees to encourage good structure

Our arborists can walk the site with you, explain what each tree needs and prioritise works according to safety, access and your budget.
